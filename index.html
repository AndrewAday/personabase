About Us

The modern recruitment process is fundamentally flawed. While it is common practice to look at resumes and skill sets, people often fail to consider the other half of an applicant--his or her personality. By incorporating established research on team composition and design, PersonaBase solves this problem.

Through algorithms that prioritize only the most compatible candidates, we enable companies to guarantee that every hire is the right one. PersonaBase is the next step to intelligent hiring.
